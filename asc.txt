1. Navigate to the AWS Management Console

2. Search for and open the 'Service Quotas' console

3. In the Service Quotas console, search for 'Amazon Elastic Compute Cloud (Amazon EC2)'

4. Look for the quota named 'Running On-Demand Standard (A, C, D, H, I, M, R, T, Z) instances'

5. Click on the 'Request increase' button for this quota

6. In the 'Increase quota value' field, specify a new vCPU limit greater than 0

7. Provide a brief explanation in the 'Justification' section for why you need more vCPUs

8. Submit the request by clicking on the 'Submit request' button

9. Wait for the approval of your request

10. If you need urgent assistance, navigate to the AWS Support Center at https://support.console.aws.amazon.com/support/home

11. Once your quota increase is approved, return to the EC2 console and attempt to launch your instance again
